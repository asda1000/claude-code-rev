# Managing your Account
> Source: /guides/account-and-subscriptions/account

Your account settings bring together everything that is personal to you, from your name and profile image to how you send messages in chats. Use this page to keep your details accurate and safe.

## **Accessing your account settings**

You can manage all your personal account options from the profile menu.

**To open your account settings:**

1. Enter the main page of CodeBanana.
2. Click the profile picture in the upper right corner.

### Change profile picture

Click on your profile picture to access the profile picture editing page. Here you can upload an image to change your profile picture.

The profile picture must meet the **following requirements**:

1. Only JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats are supported.
2. Less than 5MB.

  ![Header](/images/header.png)

### Usage

Click the "Usage" button in your personal center to view your usage details. Overall billing is divided into **two types**:

1. **Package-based requests:**

- Different membership levels—Free, Personal, and Professional—offer 30, 300, and 1000 requests respectively.
- The Personal and Professional packages allow you to choose different virtual machine options based on your service scale; billing will be based on the virtual machine service used.

2. **Pay-as-you-go**

- Once you've used up your package-based requests, you can enable pay-as-you-go billing on the Usage page, setting a limit. This will automatically trigger a Stripe billing process for top-up.

  ![Usage](/images/usage.png)

### Contacts

- You can view your organization application records, organization members, and added friends.
- Here offers a convenient search function for quickly finding people, and you can also click the chat button next to them for quick private chats.
- At this point, you will be redirected to the chat option, and all private chat history will be saved here.
